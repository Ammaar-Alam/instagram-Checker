Instagram Follower Checker

Upload your Instagram data

How to get your Instagram data:

  1. Tap your profile picture in the bottom right to go to your profile.
  2. Tap the menu in the top right, then tap Your activity.
  3. Tap Download your information.
  4. Select Followers and following from the options.
  5. Choose JSON format and All time for date range.
  6. Tap Submit request.
  7. Wait for the email with your data and download it.
