How to Download Your Instagram Data

  1. Open Instagram App and tap the profile icon in the bottom right to go to your profile.
  2. Tap the more options icon in the top right, then tap Your activity.
  3. Under Information you shared with Instagram, tap Download your information.
  4. Enter your email address where you'd like to receive the download link, then tap Request a download.
  5. Choose Select types of information and scroll down to select Followers and following.
  6. Select Format: JSON and Date range: All time, then tap Submit request.
  7. You'll receive an email titled "Your Instagram Data" with a link to download your data. This might take up to 24 hours.
